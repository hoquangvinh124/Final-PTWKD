Frequently Asked Questions

Do the devices come with warranty?

Each product comes with a minimum 6-month warranty. Refurbished devices and turntables have a 12-month warranty.

Can I inspect the item before payment?

You can schedule a visit at our showroom or request a video call to test the device. For home delivery, we'll set it up for your final inspection.

How does LDIE Zone handle shipping?

Local orders are delivered same-day by dedicated vehicle. Provincial orders are double-packed with shock-absorbing foam and fully insured.

Is there a return policy?

If a product develops technical issues within 7 days, LDIE Zone will replace it with an equivalent device or provide a full refund.
